Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our vintage car wedding rental services in Goa.

What types of vintage cars do you offer for weddings in Goa?

We offer a premium fleet including Classic Vintage Cars (₹15,000/day), Luxury Sedans (₹12,000/day), and Royal Limousines (₹25,000/day). All cars come with professional chauffeurs, wedding decorations, and are fully insured for your peace of mind.

Do you provide chauffeur service with the vintage car rental?

Yes, all our vintage car rentals include professional, courteous, and well-dressed chauffeurs. Our drivers are experienced in wedding protocols, know all major venues in Goa, and ensure a smooth, comfortable ride for your special day.

What areas in Goa do you cover for wedding car rental?

We provide vintage car rental services across all of Goa including North Goa (Calangute, Baga, Anjuna, Arambol), South Goa (Colva, Benaulim, Palolem), Panaji, Margao, Vasco, and all major wedding venues. We're available 24/7 for your convenience.

How much does vintage car rental cost for weddings in Goa?

Our pricing starts from ₹12,000 for Luxury Sedans, ₹15,000 for Classic Vintage Cars, and ₹25,000 for Royal Limousines per day (8-12 hours). All packages include professional chauffeur, fuel, basic wedding decorations, and comprehensive insurance coverage.

How far in advance should I book my wedding car rental?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 months in advance, especially during peak wedding season (November to February). However, we also accommodate last-minute bookings based on availability. Contact us at +91 9284354370 to check availability for your date.

Do you provide cars for pre-wedding photoshoots in Goa?

Absolutely! Our vintage cars are perfect for pre-wedding photoshoots. We offer flexible hourly packages and can coordinate with your photographer to visit multiple scenic locations across Goa for the perfect shots with our classic automobiles.
